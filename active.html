Meet the team that runs our chapter!

From left to right, we have our Regent, Vice Regent, Treasurer, Scribe, Corresponding Secretary, New Member Educator, and Marshal.

Marshal

The Marshal manages and enforces the national constitution and the chapter's bylaws.
